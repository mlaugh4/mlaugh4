Marvin

After a few inspiring years in data analysis, a former colleague and I explored a custom device-friendly solution. This is our result.

The main problem this solves is the often treacherous diagnostic work data analysts do every morning: How are the tests? Are all systems operating properly? How are we doing day-over-day? .. week-over-week?

Most analytics services offer strong statistical tools for robust analysis, but these ‘out-of-the-box’ solutions produce flat information with a focus on digestible presentation and easy integration. Marvin takes it a step further by helping product teams build tailored metrics dashboards that will significantly reduce first-pass problem diagnosis, freeing up resources to drive product improvement.

After a little over a month of research – speaking with various business owners, business analysts, and engineers (with experience in dashboard dev) – I had enough information on the feature set, problem space, and design to move forward with graphical mockups and design prototypes.

Interviews - market validation

Quotidian business analysis (product, marketing, financial, and competitive) requires a proximity to data trends at various ‘elevations.’ Deep analysis has the potential to unearth core performance drivers, while a ‘topographic’ perspective is more effective in strategic and development workflows. The ultimate goal is to surface what events are happening with minimal latency, understand how they influence your business, and quickly respond to them. Marvin streamlines the first step by providing a topline view of KPI fluctuations.
